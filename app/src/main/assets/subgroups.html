CACSA OAU SUBGROUPS

ACADEMIC SUBGROUP: This is the subgroup charged with the responsibility of overseeing the academic affairs of the household.
BIBLE STUDY SUBGROUP: The members of this subgroup teach the Word of God practically on Monday Bible Study meetings.
GLORIOUS FAMILY CHOIR: Members of this subgroup minister through hymns and spiritual songs with the aim of leading God’s people in the worship and praise of our God.
NEW COVENANT DRAMA MINISTRY: They are responsible for preaching the gospel and teaching the truth of God’s Word through Christian drama ministrations via stage performances.
FIRM FOUNDATION SCHOOL: A school where students are taught the basic, salient but potent truth of the Word of God and students are being followed-up to see about their spiritual growth.
FOLLOW-UP UNIT: Members are responsible for counselling, following-up and encouraging new converts and ‘first-comers’.

HEPHZIBAH SUBGROUP: The business arm of the fellowship. They execute viable business or make investment decisions to generate funds for the fellowship.
MISSIONS SUBGROUP: The members are dedicated to the proclamation of the gospel to the unreached and unsaved around us and in the distant areas. Thus namely:

  1. Campus Unit: they are involved in the preaching of the gospel on campus: in halls of residence, lecture halls, distribution of tracts, and one-on-one contact with persons.
  2. Children Unit: they minister to children in our churches or outreaches by esteeming high their salvation and godly upbringing.
  3. Hospital Unit: they bring the Word of God in salvation, healing to patients in hospitals (OAUTHC, 7th-Day Adventist, private hospitals, etc)
  4. Prison Unit: Members take the gospel to inmates and disciple them to grow unto maturity.
  5. Village Unit: Members take the gospel to people in villages i.e. off-campus. And also teach them as ministers of God.

ORGANIZING SUBGROUP: Members provides set-up such as sound and lighting in meetings.
PRAYER SUBGROUP: This is the prayer arm of the fellowship where members are intercessors for the household members.
PUBLICITY AND DECORATING SUBGROUP: They ensure the beauty of the auditorium, pulpit, etc, and also publicize meetings via the print i.e. posters, banners, artworks, inscription, etc

  1. Multimedia Unit: They are in charge of the multimedia in the fellowship.

SECRETARIAT:

  1. Editorial Unit: this unit is in charge of publications i.e. print media. It is a place for novelist, cartoonist, poets and writers.
  2. Library Unit: They are in charge of the gospel books, publications and tapes. They also lend these to members. They also see to the cleanliness of the fellowship’s secretariat.
  3. Senior Friend Relations Unit: they provide the link and bridge the gap between the household and the senior friends (alumni).

SUNDAY SCHOOL SUBGROUP: Members teach the Word of God with all authority, particularly every Sunday mornings during the Sunday school.
TRANSPORT SUBGROUP: A subgroup that cuts across all, they assist the work of God through conveying (transportation). Also it is a platform by which learners are groomed to perfect their driving skills for relevance in God’s work.
GIANT USHERING FAMILY: They ensure orderliness in the house of God (the Church), and also make sure meetings run smoothly, laudably and impeccably.
WELFARE UNIT: it is made up of all the hall representatives. They are in charge of the Saturday early morning and Sunday hall meetings. Majorly, they cater for the household.
