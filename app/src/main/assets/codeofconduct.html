CODE OF CONDUCT

The following rules apply to all students both within and outside the Firm Foundation School premises.
You are advised to strictly abide with these rules:

  • Students should not be rude to any of the teachers under any circumstance.
  • If there is any complaint, it should be directed to the Vice-principal or the Principal of the Firm Foundation School.
  • Absenteeism from three classes is paramount to withdrawal. In other words, you must not miss up to three classes as a student of the school.
  • Students are expected to always be responsible and be good ambassadors of the school.
  • Students are expected to be at the school premise on Monday by 7:30pm. Lateness will not be allowed. However due permission from your teacher (before 7:00pm) will save you from any consequence.
  • All students are expected to dress decently and responsibly:
  • For females: armless tops, tops with low neck line, trousers, loosed or uncovered hair is not allowed.
  • For males: never roll up your sleeves and always tuck in your shirt, three-quarter trousers, and body hug shirts are not allowed.
  • No native attires.
  • Under no condition would there be make-up classes except at the discretion of the Principal of the Firm Foundation School.
  • All phones should be put on silence or switched off (not in vibration)

OBEY ALL INSTRUCTIONS.
